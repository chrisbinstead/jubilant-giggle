        <p>When I don't understand what where why or how, I will ask myself the question and Chris always, always comes back with an answer...</p>
        <p>Well on a serious note, I have been dabbling with website design from using WYSIWYG editors and manipulating the code where possible for over 10 years or so. Currently I am working in IT as a general IT support technician and have been for 5 years now. Before this I was a working in the Hotel Industry for 10 years as a fully qualified Chef. I slowly grow a passion for the IT industry, as colleagues, friends and family would have questions or issues, I was able to develop enough self-taught skills to assist from a personal IT perspective.</p>
        <p> With the IT sector being such a large sphere of disciplines, from support to specializing in networking, servers, software development, web design etc... In order for me to progress I had to decide for myself which of these disciplines I wanted to specialize in, or do I go through another major career change. It took me over a year to think carefully about this.
        <p> I decided to stay in the IT sphere and specialize in Web Design. At the time of writing this, I am currently learning through Free Code Camp.</p>
